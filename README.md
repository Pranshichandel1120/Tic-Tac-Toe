# 🎮 Tic-Tac-Toe

A fully functional, interactive **Tic-Tac-Toe** game built with **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript** — play against a friend or challenge the **AI**, all in your browser with zero installation!

---

## 📖 About

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic strategy game played on a **3×3 grid**. Players take turns marking a square with their symbol — **X** or **O**. The first player to get **three marks in a row** (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. If all nine squares are filled with no winner, it's a **draw**.

This project brings the classic to life in the browser — with a clean UI, smooth gameplay, and an **AI opponent** to keep things interesting even when you're playing solo!

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 **Single-Player vs AI** — Challenge a computer opponent that plays strategically
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **Two-Player Mode** — Play against a friend on the same device
- 🏆 **Win Detection** — Automatically detects wins across all rows, columns, and diagonals
- 🤝 **Draw Detection** — Recognizes and announces a draw when the board is full
- 🔄 **Restart/Reset Button** — Instantly reset the board for a new round
- ✅ **Turn Indicator** — Clearly shows whose turn it is at all times
- 🌟 **Winning Highlight** — Highlights the winning combination on the board
- 📱 **Responsive Design** — Works smoothly on desktop, tablet, and mobile

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose your mode — **Single Player (vs AI)** or **Two Player**
2. The game starts with **Player X** going first
3. Click on any **empty cell** to place your mark
4. Players (or the AI) **alternate turns** after each move
5. The game announces a **winner** when a player gets three in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
6. If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, the game declares a **Draw**
7. Click the **Restart** button to play again
